ABOUT US

“Nirav S. Malkan & Co.” is a Tax & Legal advisory firm. We cater to an extremely wide spectrum of prestigious companies. Each of the service division has a specialised team, thus enabling a dedicated service offering for Audit & Accounting, Direct & Indirect Taxes, International Transactions, FEMA and niche offerings – all of these under a single roof with respective specializations.

Our philosophy is of partnering with our clients and not being a distant service provider. Since businesses are inherently different, we tailor our services to meet client’s specific needs and banish the ‘one-size-fits-all’ standardisation

KEY PERSON

CA Nirav Malkan

CA Nirav Malkan is a founder of the firm. He is a graduate and a member of Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. He has wide experience in the field of Direct Tax and Indirect Tax.

He has been instrumental in advising clients on tax planning and tax compliance with the highest standards of ethics and client satisfaction. The timely response to the tax authorities as well as to the queries of the clients make him an often sought for personality at the office. His unending spirit of learning and developing new techniques is always appreciated and beneficial to the firm. His areas of specialisation are corporate tax planning & compliance thereof, International tax matters relating to interpretation of tax treaties and attestation of Transfer Pricing Reports, advisory in goods and services tax, GST audit, assessment etc.

He has served various multinational companies and listed companies. He has been a catalyst in expanding the portfolio of the firm’s services to multi-faceted consultancy.



Mr. Snehal Thakkar

Mr. Snehal Thakkar has extensive experience in representing varied Domestic and International Tax matters before various tax authorities including Commissioner Appeals, Additional & Deputy commissioner, Members of Tax Tribunal & Settlement Commission in additions to normal tax matters & tax audits with a forte in the field on Indirect Taxes. He has handled special assignments of direct and indirect Tax as well as search & survey matters.

He has unparalleled taxation experience across variety of industrial sectors with a knack of handling complex litigation matters with utmost client satisfaction including advisory on matters involving direct and indirect tax implications for restructuring of sick companies, insolvent companies and companies under liquidation. He is also a past president of Tax Bar Association, Ahmedabad. His association and guidance to the firm makes value addition to clients.
